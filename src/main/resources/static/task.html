Notary Office

The activity of the notary office is organized as follows: the firm is ready to provide the client with a certain set of services. To keep things organized, you have to formalize these services by listing them with a description of each service. When a client contacts you, his standard data (name, type of activity, address, telephone) are recorded in the database. A table is drawn up for each fact of service provision to the client. The table indicates the service, the amount of payment for the agreement, commissions (office income), description of the agreement.

Extension of the statement of the task

Several services may be provided to the client within the single agreement. The cost of each service is fixed. In addition, the company provides various types of discounts within the single agreement. Discounts can be combined.
